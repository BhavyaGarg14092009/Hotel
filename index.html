Radisson Jass Shimla

We are located in hearts of Shimla

Situated in the Himalaya Mountains, Radisson Jass Shimla boasts a convenient location near the historic city center and inspiring views from our rooms and suites. Stay just minutes from popular area attractions like the Gaiety Heritage Cultural Complex, Jakhu Temple, and Christ Church. Spend the afternoon touring the former Viceregal Lodge at the Indian Institute of Advanced Study or shopping for souvenirs at Shimla's popular Mall. As an added convenience, Shimla Airport (SLV) is only 26 kilometers away from our hotel.
